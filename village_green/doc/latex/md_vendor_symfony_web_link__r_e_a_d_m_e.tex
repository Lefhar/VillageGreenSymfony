The Web\+Link component manages links between resources. It is particularly useful to advise clients to preload and prefetch documents through HTTP and HTTP/2 pushes.

This component implements the \href{https://www.w3.org/TR/html5/links.html}{\texttt{ HTML5\textquotesingle{}s Links}}, \href{https://www.w3.org/TR/preload/}{\texttt{ Preload}} and \href{https://www.w3.org/TR/resource-hints/}{\texttt{ Resource Hints}} W3C\textquotesingle{}s specifications.
